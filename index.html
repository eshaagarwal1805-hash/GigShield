Protecting India’s Gig Workforce with AI-Driven Safety

GigShield is a digital safety companion designed to support gig workers through real-time monitoring, financial transparency, and community-driven protection systems.

The Problem

India’s gig economy is expected to reach nearly 50 million workers by 2026. Despite this growth, gig workers frequently face payment disputes, unsafe working environments, harassment, and lack of social security. Many accidents during work hours go uncompensated and workers often lack reliable systems for real-time support or reporting issues.

How GigShield Helps

Real-Time Safety Monitoring

Workers can log shifts with geolocation check-ins and enable safety tracking, ensuring their work sessions are recorded and monitored for risk.

Emergency SOS Alerts

In dangerous situations, workers can trigger instant SOS alerts to unions, NGOs, or trusted contacts through push notifications and SMS.

AI Risk Detection

Machine learning identifies unusual earnings patterns, frequent disputes, or safety incidents to help workers detect risks early.

Community Support Forums

Anonymous peer forums allow workers to share experiences, report harassment, and warn others about unsafe areas.

Payout Verification

Employer-uploaded payment data is compared with worker logs to ensure fair compensation and transparency.

Safety Heatmaps

Community safety reports create real-time maps highlighting high-risk zones across cities.
